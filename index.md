I am currently a research developer at [Center for Language and Speech Processing](https://www.clsp.jhu.edu/) in Johns Hopkins University,
where I work with [Mark Dredze](http://www.cs.jhu.edu/~mdredze) on Computational Social Science and Natural Language Processing.
Especially, I used Geotagged Tweets to investigate how people are practicing social distancing in United States,
including demographics analysis. I am also working on e cigarettes brands discovery from online forums.
I graduated from Johns Hopkins University with M.S.E in Computer Science.

Previously, I have worked with [Yong Deng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8voeILsAAAAJ&hl=en) on Complex Network,
and [Tao Zhou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=MXgWgmEAAAAJ&hl=en) on Human Mobility.

**I am applying for PhD starting at Fall 2021.**
